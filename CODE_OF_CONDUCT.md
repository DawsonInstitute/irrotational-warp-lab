# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

This project is a research-oriented codebase. We want a welcoming, constructive environment.

We expect all participants to:
- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical substance.
- Provide actionable feedback (reproduction steps, logs, minimal examples).
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive personal information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile conduct.
- Sexualized or violent language directed at others.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussion or collaboration.

## Reporting

If something goes wrong, please open an issue (if appropriate) or contact the maintainer(s) privately.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to keep the project safe and productive.
